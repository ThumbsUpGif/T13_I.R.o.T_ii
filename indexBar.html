Our story

For too long Torshov has lived under the yoke of Sagene Bydel, the city of Oslo and the Kingdom of Norway. Since times immemorial the proud people of Torshov has sought complete freedom to forge their own path. Following the model of successful micro-nations such as Monaco and Singapore, The Independent Republic of Torshov is set to become an outsized player in the international arena. Having followed a policy of peaceful resistance since the 1990s our struggle for freedom is now focused on a constructive public discourse and a well balanced menu of cocktails and craft beers.
