The story of our struggle

For too long Torshov has lived under the yoke of Sagene Bydel, the city of Oslo and the Kingdom of Norway. Since times immemorial the proud people of Torshov have wanted freedom to manage their own affairs. Building on the successful model of micro-nations such as Monaco and Singapore, the Independent Republic of Torshov is destined to one day become a valued member of the global community. After committing to a policy of peaceful resistance since the 1990s, our struggle for freedom is now focused on building a constructive public discourse over a well balanced menu of cocktails and craft beers.
